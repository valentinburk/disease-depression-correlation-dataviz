Diseases and Depression Rates in 2019

This visualization shows the relationship between various diseases and depression rates in the United States from 2019 to 2022. Each circle represents a disease, with its position indicating the disease's prevalence (x-axis) and associated depression rate (y-axis).

  • Use the "Previous Year" and "Next Year" buttons to navigate between years.
  • Click on any disease circle to see a detailed breakdown of factors contributing to that disease.
  • Hover over circles for more information.

This tool allows you to explore how different health conditions correlate with depression rates and how these relationships have changed over time.

Data source: U.S. Chronic Disease Indicators
